Quick Start Guide

Get your Employee Management System up and running in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start managing your team through Telegram.

  • ← Back to Home
  • Installation Guide
  • API Reference
  • FAQ
1

Find the Bot

Start by finding our Employee Management Bot on Telegram. You can search for it or use the direct link provided by your administrator.

Search for: @YourEMSBot Or click: https://t.me/your_bot_username
Note: Make sure you're using the official bot. Verify the username with your administrator to avoid scams.
2

Start the Bot

Once you've found the bot, click the "Start" button or send the /start command to begin the registration process.

/start

The bot will greet you and guide you through the initial setup process. If you're a new user, you'll be prompted to register your company.

3

Register Your Company

Follow the bot's prompts to register your company:

  1. Company Name: Enter your company's full name
  2. Subscription Plan: Choose from our available plans:
    • 1 Month - $29.99
    • 6 Months - $149.99 (Save $30!)
    • Lifetime - $499.99 (Best Value!)
Pro Tip: The 6-month plan offers the best balance of savings and flexibility for most businesses.
4

Complete Payment

After selecting your plan, you'll receive a secure payment link. Complete the payment to activate your subscription.

  • Click the payment link provided by the bot
  • Enter your payment details securely
  • Complete the transaction
  • Wait for confirmation (usually instant)
Security: All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels. We never store your payment information.
5

Activation & Setup

Once payment is confirmed:

  1. The system administrator will receive a notification
  2. Your subscription will be activated (usually within minutes)
  3. You'll receive a confirmation message
  4. The main menu will become available
You're Ready! Your Employee Management System is now active and ready to use.
6

Add Your First Employee

Now let's add your first employee to the system:

  1. Tap ➕ Add Employee from the main menu
  2. Follow the prompts to enter employee information:
    • Employee ID (unique identifier)
    • First and Last Name
    • Designation/Job Title
    • Contact Information
    • Joining Date
    • Salary (optional)
    • Department
  3. Review the information and confirm
Example Employee Entry: Employee ID: EMP001 Name: John Doe Designation: Senior Developer Phone: +1-555-0123 Email: john.doe@company.com Joining Date: 2024-01-15 Salary: 75000 Department: Engineering

Tips for Success

  • Use consistent Employee ID formats (e.g., EMP001, EMP002)

  • Regularly backup important data

  • Use the search function for large teams

  • Train your HR team on the system
